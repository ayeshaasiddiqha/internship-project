Operating System

Operating systems are essential software that manage hardware resources and enable user interaction with computers. They facilitate tasks like file management, memory allocation, and device communication. Examples include Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, and iOS, each tailored to specific devices and user needs. Understanding operating systems is crucial for students and educators alike, as they form the foundation of computing environments, influencing efficiency, security, and usability in educational settings and beyond.
